MyAgricensus Portal

The Agriculture Census 2024 portal is the best medium to reach out to the community, and as the main medium to deliver the important information of the Agriculture Statistics. The comprehensive and interactive content of the portal will serve as an information gateway that provides a lot of information in the form of infographics and interactive visualisations. MyAgricensus Portal also provides a chatbot platform, where users can communicate in real time for any agriculture statistics information needed.

Economic Census 2023 Portal

Economic Census portal is a common gateway with a one-stop centre concept to make it easier for users, especially census respondents, to get all the information related to the implementation of the 2023 Economic Census. Among the contents of this portal are general information about the 2023 Economic Census, frequently asked questions (FAQ), questionnaires, guide to complete questionnaire, the previous publications of economic census, and links to online questionnaires (e-Be). Through this portal, users can also access economic data dashboard (MyEase) which covers economic series data obtained from censuses and economic surveys.

MyCensus 2020 Portal

In line with the technological advancements and lifestyle changes of Malaysians, online census information collection is being introduced through the Census Portal and mobile applications. To complete the e-Census form, a postcard containing the e-Census guide and link on the Census Portal will be sent to your residence. In addition, mobile applications are also introduced for enumerators to fill out your information during interviews using a tablet or smartphone.

DOSM GeoHUB

This data portal was developed to facilitate access to geospatial information integrated with the nation's official statistics. This platform supports planning and development at the national, state, and administrative district levels through the provision of more organized, easily comprehensible, and location-based online data delivery. DOSM GEOHUB is also a crucial initiative towards government digital services through the provision of digital mapping and spatial analytics to all users.

Malaysia Statistical Training Institute (ILSM)

The Institute was inspired more than 20 years ago. The development of in-house training programmes in the Department of Statistics, Malaysia has started in 1987, with the formation of a Working Group of Human Resource Development. In 1990, the Statistical Development Division has been established and the Division was responsible for providing in-house training programmes. Following from this, the Statistical Training Division has been established in 2003 with 21 staffs.

TaniStats Portal

The Integrated Agricultural Statistical System (TaniStats) is a digital system that organizes and simplifies agricultural data. It brings together information from different sources, making it easier to monitor, analyze, and plan for a stronger and more sustainable agricultural sector.

ISI WSC 2019 Portal

The ISI WSC 2019 portal serves as a reference and documentation platform for the International Statistical Institute World Statistics Congress (ISI WSC) 2019. Through this site, visitors can access a variety of materials including presentation papers, photo galleries of program activities, and reference resources for future researchers and participants. The aim is to preserve the outcomes and memories of the congress as a valuable source of knowledge and guidance for future events.
